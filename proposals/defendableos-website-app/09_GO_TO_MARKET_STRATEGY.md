## THE BOARD'S QUESTION

## THE LOCKED ANSWER

> *"Do not publish a public price first."*
>
> *"Run a controlled call-for-offers process with qualified buyers only."*

This is the classic Marcus & Millichap industrial-CRE disposition technique applied to AI-defense category-defining assets. The principal (Mr. DefendableOS) used this method to close $8B in CRE transactions across 30 years. We apply it here.

## WHY NOT PUBLIC PRICE · the 4 risks of commoditization

A published public price attracts:

| Vendor Type | What They Bid | What They Miss |
|---|---|---|
| **Generic web shops** | Pages and features | The trust architecture · the doctrine · the receipts |
| **Low-context dev agencies** | Templates and stack work | Founder voice · operator vocabulary · the books-and-records framing |
| **AI SaaS template builders** | Reusable SaaS components | The category-definition opportunity · the proof-layer thesis |
| **Vendors who bid features · not trust architecture** | Lowest-cost feature list | The Class A 5-cap result · the trust-infrastructure pricing |

**Public price = noise = weakens the asset.**

## WHY CALL FOR OFFERS · the 6 advantages of controlled bidding

A controlled call-for-offers process lets us test:

- Who **understands the doctrine** (operator vocabulary · 5 Swarm Laws · PASS · MAGIC)
- Who **can build** Router / Tribunal / receipts / object storage / DDEED rails (real systems competence)
- Who **can preserve founder language** (V03 validator chain pass)
- Who has **real systems ability** (not just front-end pages)
- Who can **show proof of execution** (case studies · prior comparable work)
- Who can **close in the 24-hour window** (operational discipline · responsiveness)

**Controlled process = signal = strengthens the asset's pricing power.**

## HIGHEST-AND-BEST PROFILE · the 8 qualifications (NOT lowest bidder)

The most qualified bidder is NOT the lowest bid. They must demonstrate:

1. **Full-stack app experience**

3. **Object storage + JSON receipt architecture**

## THE 3-ROUND CALL-FOR-OFFERS PROCESS

### Round 1 · Quiet Market (T+0 to T+10)

- Send flight sheet to **3-5 handpicked firms / builders** (PRIVATE · not public listing)
- Provide:
  - Assignment summary
  - Doctrine extract
  - Required architecture
  - Comp set
  - Acceptance criteria
  - Response deadline (T+10)
- Bidders respond by T+10 with all 9 required items (see below)

### Round 2 · Best and Final (T+10 to T+20)

- Shortlist to **2 finalists** based on Round 1 evaluation rubric
- Require finalists to submit:
  - Proposed architecture
  - Build timeline
  - Fee structure
  - Team bios
  - Proof of similar work
  - Risk flags
  - First 14-day delivery plan
- Finalists respond by T+20

### Round 3 · Award (T+20 to T+30)

- Award to the builder with the **strongest combination** of:
  - Understanding
  - Proof
  - Speed
  - Architecture
  - Trust
  - Price discipline
- Award announced T+25 · LOU executed T+30 · Hedera anchor on award decision

## PRICING STRATEGY · guided range · NOT fixed public ask

Bidders price each phase separately. Prevents one foggy giant number. Lets board compare cleanly.

| Phase | Scope | Pricing Discipline |
|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1** | Board-ready public site + app shell | **FIXED price required** |
| **Phase 2** | Router + receipts + object storage | **RANGE estimate** ($X-$Y) |
| **Phase 3** | Tribunal + DDEED viewer + trust dials | **RANGE estimate** |
| **Phase 4** | StreetChat / StreetLedger / Communicator integration | **RANGE estimate** |

**Why phased**: each phase is a natural milestone gate · Owner retains optionality at each phase · winning bidder must re-earn each phase via delivery quality.

### 5 · Reserve / Walk-Away Discipline (PASS doctrine applied to bidder selection)

**Automatic walk triggers** (Broker exercises without Owner consultation):
- No bidder meets 60% cut score → re-issue with refined bidder pool
- All bidders fail Founder voice preservation (V03 validator) → walk regardless
- Bidder requests Owner direct contact during silent period → bidder disqualified
- Bidder shares confidential docs externally → disqualified + NDA enforcement
- Best bid exceeds reserve ceiling (to be set with Owner before Round 1) → walk + re-issue

**Re-issue option**: if all 3 firms in first cycle are walked-from · Broker expands pool to 5-7 firms for second cycle with refined doctrine extract.

### 6 · Phased Award Structure (matches phased pricing)

Award binds bidder to Phase 1 fixed price ONLY. Phase 2-4 negotiated as separate scope expansions at each milestone:

- Phase 1 award + 6-month exclusive option on Phase 2-4
- Phase 2 negotiated at Phase 1 close (uses Phase 1 delivery quality as input · failed Phase 1 = walk)
- Phase 3 negotiated at Phase 2 close · same gating
- Phase 4 negotiated at Phase 3 close · same gating

**Owner optionality preserved**: if winning bidder underperforms on Phase 1 · Owner can revoke Phase 2-4 option and re-bid those phases · without breach.
