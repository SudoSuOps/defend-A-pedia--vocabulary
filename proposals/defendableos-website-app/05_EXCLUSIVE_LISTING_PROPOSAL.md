## EXCLUSIVE LISTING TERMS · standard industrial CRE structure

### Exclusivity Period: 12 months
- Single broker on the deal (us)
- Owner does NOT list with other brokers during exclusivity
- ALL inbound buyer inquiries route through us (we handle qualification · owner doesn't field calls)

### Tail / Protection Period: 12 months post-expiration
- If a buyer was first introduced or substantively engaged during exclusivity, AND that buyer closes within 12 months of expiration → commission applies
- Standard industrial CRE protection · prevents owner-side end-runs
- Documented buyer list maintained throughout exclusivity for audit clarity

### Commission Structure: sliding scale at closing

| Deal Type | Commission | Commission Floor | Commission Cap |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Full company sale (M&A)** | **5% of enterprise value** | $250K | No cap |
| **Strategic partnership / JV** | **6% of 5-year deal value** | $150K | $2M |
| **IP license (enterprise)** | **8% of 3-year license value** | $100K | $1M |
| **Spin-out / carve-out** | **6% of asset value** | $200K | No cap |
| **Other deal structures** | TBD per addendum to rep agreement | TBD | TBD |

- **Floor** = minimum commission regardless of deal size (justifies broker's pre-listing build investment)
- **Cap** = maximum commission (caps owner's exposure on outsized deals · fair to owner)
- All commissions paid AT CLOSING via wire to broker · per rep agreement

### Marketing Budget Allowance: $50,000 (cap)
- Owner reimburses out-of-pocket marketing expenses up to $50K
- Eligible: OM printing · video production · paid distribution · third-party data subscriptions for buyer outreach · conference travel for direct buyer meetings
- **NOT eligible** (broker absorbs): website build · app build · DDEED system completion · staff time · in-house design
- Reimbursed at closing OR amortized monthly across the close (broker's choice)
- If no close within exclusivity · broker eats the marketing spend entirely

### Listing Premium Discount: 15% commission reduction
- If owner directly introduces the closing buyer (vs broker-procured)
- Recognizes Mr. DefendableOS's 30-year CRE network
- Documented in the rep agreement · applies automatically

### Performance Gates: owner protections
- **6-month gate**: if no qualified offer at ≥75% of BOV value → owner may terminate without penalty
- **9-month gate**: if no LOI executed → owner may terminate without penalty
- **Termination**: owner retains ALL build work product (website · app · DDEED system) at no additional cost · broker eats the build investment
- Standard CRE owner protections · no Hail Mary brokerage
