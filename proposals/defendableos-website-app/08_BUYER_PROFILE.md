## WHY THEY BUY

Buyers buy DefendableOS because AI agents are moving from **"assistant"** to **"operator,"** and once agents touch real workflows, failure becomes business risk.

They buy to **avoid**:

- Agent failure
- Silent hallucination
- Bad customer decisioning
- Unverified outputs
- Missing audit trail
- No proof of process
- No repair history
- No books and records

## THE REAL BUY REASON (the trigger sentence)

> *"We are using AI in the business · but we cannot prove what it did · why it did it · whether it succeeded · or what happened when it failed."*

When a prospect says ANY version of that sentence · they are pre-qualified. PASS doctrine → engage immediately.

**DefendableOS sells the PROOF LAYER.**

## BROKER OUTREACH ENGINE (added by broker · Stream 3 execution layer)

The founder's response is buyer doctrine. The broker's job is to execute it. Below is the execution layer.

### Persona-sequencing matrix · two close paths

| Path | Sequence | Cycle Time |
|---|---|---|
| **Founder-led close** | Founder → AI Product Owner (validates stack) → CFO (approves spend) → GC (reviews terms) → COO (operationalizes) | 60-90 days |
| **Enterprise-led close** | AI Product Owner (gets us in door) → COO (operational risk ack) → GC (compliance gate) → CFO (budget gate) → Founder (signs check) | 120-180 days |

Broker reads which sequence applies per target during initial discovery.

### Channel-by-persona outreach matrix

| Persona | Primary Channel | Secondary Channel | Cycle Speed |
|---|---|---|---|
| Founder | Direct warm intro · podcast | Founder Twitter/X | Fastest |
| COO | Operations conferences | Peer COO referral | Mid |
| GC / Compliance | Regulatory events · law firm intro | Compliance officer summit | Slower |
| CFO | CFO peer network (FEI · AFP) | Audit firm referral | Slowest |
| AI Product Owner | LinkedIn · GitHub · HuggingFace | AI eng conferences (NeurIPS · MLSys) | Mid-tech |

### PASS doctrine filters (apply at every discovery call)

**Filter OUT** · do not invest broker time:
- Tire-kickers ("just exploring AI")
- Academic interest (no budget · no timeline · no documented risk exposure)
- Vendors-of-vendors (they want to white-label · not buy)
- Pre-revenue startups (no books-and-records to defend yet)
- Companies with no AI in production (premature buyer)

**Filter IN** · engage hard:
- Budget authority confirmed
- Timeline expressed (3-6 month horizon)
- Documented AI risk exposure (prior incident · near-miss · regulatory letter · customer complaint about agent behavior)
- Principal-level sponsor (Founder/COO/GC direct involvement)
- Existing AI spend ≥ $100K/year (proves agents are real · not theoretical)

### Per-persona OM messaging deltas (broker tailors per call)

| Persona | OM Lead-In |
|---|---|
| Founder | *"If your agents go dark · this is the layer that says what they did · why they did it · and what you can defend in front of a customer or court."* |
| COO | *"Your workflows now have an audit trail you didn't have to build · with an escalation path you control."* |
| GC | *"Every AI decision is deeded · hashed · and anchored on a public chain · before anyone subpoenas the records."* |
