## 2 · TERM AND EXCLUSIVITY

**2.1 Exclusivity Period**: 12 months from Effective Date.

**2.2 Tail / Protection Period**: 12 months following expiration or termination of this Agreement. If a buyer was first introduced or substantively engaged by Broker during the Exclusivity Period (per documented buyer list maintained by Broker), commission per Section 4 applies if such buyer closes within the Tail Period.

**2.3 Single Broker**: During the Exclusivity Period, Owner shall not engage any other broker, agent, finder, or intermediary for the Asset. All buyer inquiries shall be routed to Broker for qualification.

**2.4 Buyer List**: Broker shall maintain a written log of all buyers contacted, qualified, or substantively engaged. This list is the basis for Tail Period commission claims and shall be shared with Owner monthly.

| Spin-out / carve-out | 6% of asset value | $200,000 | No cap |
| Other deal structures | TBD per addendum | TBD | TBD |

**4.2 Listing Premium Discount**: If Owner directly introduces the closing buyer (vs Broker-procured), commission is reduced by 15%. Direct-introduction status determined by the Buyer List maintained per Section 2.4.

**4.3 Commission Payment**: All commissions paid via wire transfer at closing, simultaneous with Owner's receipt of deal proceeds, from escrow per the PSA.

**4.4 No Hourly Fees**: Broker shall not invoice Owner for hourly work, project management, build labor, design, or any other professional services rendered. ALL such work is included in Broker's listing investment, recoverable only via commission at closing.

**4.5 No Up-Front Fees**: Owner shall pay no retainer, no engagement fee, no setup fee, and no milestone invoices to Broker.

## 7 · PERFORMANCE GATES

**7.1 6-Month Gate**: If no qualified offer at ≥75% of the BOV value has been received within 6 months of the Effective Date, Owner may terminate this Agreement without penalty.

**7.2 9-Month Gate**: If no executed LOI has been received within 9 months of the Effective Date, Owner may terminate this Agreement without penalty.

**7.3 Termination Effect**: Upon performance-gate termination:
- Owner retains ALL build work product (website · app · DDEED system completions) at no additional cost
- Broker forfeits all marketing budget reimbursement
- Broker forfeits all commission (no Tail per Section 2.2 on performance-gate termination)
- Owner is free to engage other brokers immediately

**7.4 Non-Performance Gate Termination**: Owner may terminate at any time for material breach by Broker (notice and cure provisions apply per Section 10).
