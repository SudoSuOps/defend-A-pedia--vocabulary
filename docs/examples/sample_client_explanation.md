## What we do for you

## How we do it

Three things · running 24/7.

1. **The Router** · sits in front of your agents · writes a receipt for every single decision (input · output · cost · model · timestamp · hash). Adds less than 5 milliseconds. Captures · doesn't gate.

2. **The Tribunal** · grades every receipt against a defined rubric. Pass / fail / repair-candidate. Two-judge consensus. Drift-checked.

3. **The SwarmFixer** · when something fails · the fixer diagnoses the root cause · proposes the repair · re-tests · re-grades. The receipt trail proves the recovery.

Every graded output becomes a permanent record (a "Defendable Agent Deed") anchored on Hedera HCS. The record is yours · forever · auditable by any third party with the topic ID.
