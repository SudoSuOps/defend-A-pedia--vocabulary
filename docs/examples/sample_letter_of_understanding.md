## §1 · Purpose

This Letter of Understanding ("LOU") establishes the engagement between Defender and Principal for the provision of 24/7 AI defense services covering the Principal's production AI agent fleet. This LOU governs all assignments executed under it. Assignment-specific terms are documented in individual Assignment Briefs appended to this LOU as exhibits.

This is not a SaaS subscription. This is a broker-grade defense engagement governed by the Defender's published operating doctrine at https://defendableos.com/doctrine.

## §3 · Success Criteria Framework

All assignments under this LOU are subject to the Assignment Success Doctrine (https://defendableos.com/doctrine/14_assignment_success_doctrine).

### 3.1 · Boolean Outcomes

Each closed assignment will be reported as one of:
- **CLOSED · HONEY** · composite score ≥ 0.85 · all 5 grades ≥ 0.70
- **CLOSED · CONDITIONED** · composite ≥ 0.70 · 4 of 5 grades passing
- **FAILED · RECOVERABLE** · composite 0.50-0.69 · repair-candidate
- **FAILED · DARK** · composite < 0.50 OR critical Propolis event · triggers §7

### 3.2 · 5-Grade Breakdown

Every closing statement reports all 5 grades:

| Grade | Weight |
|---|---|
| G1 · Outcome | 30% |
| G2 · Truth | 25% |
| G3 · Safety | 20% |
| G4 · Economics | 15% |
| G5 · Defensibility | 10% |

G3 < 0.70 OR any Propolis event on a critical path caps the assignment at FAILED regardless of composite.

## §4 · Economics

### 4.1 · Cost-to-Mint Basis

Cost-to-mint is the Defender's all-in per-deed cost on owned compute. Current published baseline: **$0.0052 USD per Royal Jelly deed** (re-derivable per the 7-component formula at https://defendableos.com/economics).

### 4.2 · Tier Multiplier and Ceiling

Principal pays the cost-to-mint multiplied by the tier multiplier · capped at the contracted ceiling.

| Item | Value |
|---|---|
| T3 multiplier | 8x |
| Effective rate ceiling | **$0.0416 USD per deed** |
| Buffer for formula changes | +10% (changes inside this buffer do not require renegotiation) |
| Monthly floor | **$8,500 USD** (runs the apparatus regardless of deed count) |
| Volume break (>50,000 deeds/mo) | 7% reduction on effective rate |

### 4.3 · Invoicing

- Monthly · arrears
- Itemized per-assignment · per-deed-class
- Closing statement appended to monthly invoice for any assignment closed in the billing period
- Hedera HCS anchor cited for each deed referenced
- Variance reporting vs ceiling required on every invoice

### 4.4 · No Silent Meter Changes

Cost-to-mint formula updates are issued as deeds (`DDEED-DOV-ECON-Q{n}-{year}-v{v}`) on the published quarterly schedule. Out-of-cycle changes are allowed only for the three documented triggers: GPU efficiency · Hedera fee schedule · validator chain composition. Changes outside the buffer require Principal acknowledgment in writing before they hit the invoice.

## §5 · Operating Cadence

### 5.1 · Morning Brief

Defender delivers a Morning Brief to Principal-designated recipients (see §11) at **06:00 ET each calendar day**, including weekends and holidays unless explicitly paused by Principal request.

The Morning Brief includes:
- Probability of Close dial · band · 3-driver summary · 1-action item
- 24-hour deed volume · Honey rate · Propolis count
- Cost-to-mint actual (rolling 7-day)
- Any open escalation status

### 5.2 · Tribunal Cadence

- Continuous receipt grading via 2-pass Tribunal (Scale A + Scale B)
- 2am nightly reconciliation cron · Daily Reconciliation Deed issued by 06:00
- Weekly sr-broker review · published in Friday Morning Brief
- Monthly Defendable Closing Statement for each assignment milestone
- Quarterly Business Review (QBR) · in-person or video at Principal's election

### 5.3 · Escalation Pyramid

| Trigger | Owner | Response time |
|---|---|---|
| Probability of Close drop > 0.10 in 24 hrs | Sr broker | Same day · voice call |
| Drop into YELLOW band | Sr broker | Same day · voice call |
| Drop into ORANGE band | Founder + sr broker | 48 hours · revised flight sheet |
| Drop into RED band | Founder leads | Same day · phone available |
| Critical Propolis event | Founder cc · sr broker drives | 24 hours |
| Cost-to-mint > 110% ceiling | Founder cc | 24 hours · variance explanation |

## §7 · Failure Handling

### 7.1 · FAILED · DARK Protocol

On any FAILED · DARK closing or any critical Propolis on a critical path:

1. Same-day principal call (founder available)
2. Failure deed issued (`DDEED-DOV-LOGISTICS-ACME-FAIL-{seq}-v1`) within 24 hours
3. Repair plan presented within 72 hours
4. Fee abatement options offered per §7.2

### 7.2 · Fix-or-Refund (T3 White-Glove Inclusion)

On any FAILED · DARK outcome attributable to Defender (per Tribunal-graded root-cause analysis):

- 90-day Fix window from the failure deed date
- If unfixed within 90 days · Principal may elect:
  - (a) Pro-rata refund of the assignment fee · OR
  - (b) Conversion of equivalent fee to a future-credit at 1.25× face value

Defender's election does not waive principal's right to terminate per §9.

---

## §8 · PASS Doctrine

Defender operates under the published PASS Doctrine: Defender refuses fantasy mandates and will decline assignments that cannot be made defendable.

Principal acknowledges that Defender retains the right to:
- Decline new assignment briefs that fail the PASS check
- Pause in-flight assignments that have crossed the PASS threshold during execution
- Recommend PASS-pivot conversations when an engagement drifts into structural infeasibility

A PASS-pivot does NOT trigger the Fix-or-Refund clause. PASS protects both sides.

---

## §9 · Term and Termination

### 9.1 · Initial Term

**12 months** from Effective Date · auto-renewing for additional 12-month periods unless either party gives 60-day written notice.

### 9.2 · Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate immediately for material breach. Material breach includes:

- (Defender) failure to meet 99.9% deed-issuance SLA for 2 consecutive months
- (Defender) silent meter change in violation of §4.4
- (Defender) failure to escalate per §5.3 on 3 or more documented triggers
- (Principal) non-payment of undisputed invoice exceeding 60 days
- (Principal) insertion of unaudited agents into the covered fleet without amendment

### 9.3 · Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate without cause on 60-day notice. Defender will:

- Complete all in-flight assignments
- Issue closing statements for each
- Anchor final deed batch on Hedera
- Hand over read-only access to books-and-records (7-year retention preserved)

## §10 · Confidentiality and Data

### 10.1 · No Training Without Consent

Defender will NOT train Defender-owned models on Principal's data without explicit written consent per individual data class.

### 10.2 · Receipt Metadata

Receipt metadata (hashes · costs · timestamps · model versions) is the Defender's operating record and is NOT considered Principal confidential information. Raw inputs and outputs ARE Principal confidential information and remain Principal property.

### 10.3 · Aggregated Statistics

Defender may publish aggregated · anonymized statistics across the customer book (e.g., "median Honey rate across logistics-vertical engagements") without Principal identification.
