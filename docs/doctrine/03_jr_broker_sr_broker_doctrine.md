# Jr Broker · Sr Broker Doctrine

> *"No ideas · in the pit · make the dials · build the math · now your conversion rates · eat fees."*
> — The M&M operator chant

This is the career ladder. Two roles. Clear ownership. Clear escalation. The same structure that built Marcus & Millichap into the 80% market share leader of $2M-$10M CRE deals · now applied to AI defense engagements.

There is no "manager." There is no "individual contributor." There is the jr broker in the pit · and the sr broker on the listings. Every operator in DefendableOS starts in one role and earns into the other. The grass-roots ladder is the brand.

---

## The two roles, locked

**Jr Broker · microscaler · the dial.** Owns the M and the A of MAGIC. Meetings and Appraisals. Builds pipeline. Builds color. Drafts the pre-market flight sheet. Prepares the digest. Runs the validator chain first pass. Logs the receipt. Owns the leading indicators · dials per day, sits booked, color files opened, validator runs cleared.

**Sr Broker · sr judge · the listing.** Owns the I and the C of MAGIC. Ink and Close. Adjudicates. Wins the listing. Signs the LOU. Authorizes the deed. Carries validator override authority. Carries PASS doctrine authority. Owns the lagging indicators · listings won, deeds issued, fees eaten, customers retained.

Together they run the MAGIC funnel. Apart, neither works.

---

## What the jr broker owns

### Daily ownership (the pit)
- **Dial volume.** Cold outreach. Warm intros from the founder's network. Inbound from `defendableos.com`. Target: 40-60 quality dials per day · not 200 vanity dials. Quality means researched · named decision-maker · pain hypothesis ready.
- **Color building.** Every active engagement gets a color file. Three independent sources minimum. EDGAR/CompStak/ATTOM-grade evidence weighted at 0.85+. The color file feeds `engagement.color_score`. See [`../vocabulary/cre_terms/color.md`](sample).
- **Digest preparation.** Every customer meeting gets a digest delivered to the sr broker 24 hours before the sit. The digest is the pre-meeting summary the agent prepares. No digest · no sit. See [`../vocabulary/cre_terms/digest.md`](../vocabulary/cre_terms/digest.md).
- **Validator chain first pass.** Run the 12-check chain. Surface anything that fails the 7 critical checks. Flag the 5 advisory checks. The jr broker does not adjudicate · they surface.
- **Receipt logging.** Every conversation, every email, every demo · receipted into the books. The receipt is the audit trail. The audit trail is the moat.

### Weekly ownership
- **Pipeline rebuild.** Every Monday morning, the jr broker rebuilds the pipeline view. Stage-by-stage conversion rates calculated. Drops since last week explained. Adds since last week sourced.
- **MAGIC funnel math.** Meetings booked vs target. Appraisals delivered vs sits. Ink rate this week vs trailing 4. The math is the math · the dials don't lie.
- **Color refresh.** Any color file older than 14 days gets a refresh pass. Stale color is worse than no color · it lies confidently.

### Quarterly ownership
- **Vocabulary contribution.** Every jr broker is expected to surface one new term per quarter from live conversations. New term gets drafted in `docs/vocabulary/cre_terms/` and submitted for sr broker review. This is how the language grows.
- **Vertical specialization.** By quarter 4, every jr broker has a declared area of specialization · industrial defense · medical defense · cold-storage defense · etc. M&M parallel. The specialization is what compounds into a book of business.

## What the sr broker owns

### Daily ownership (the listings)
- **Adjudication.** Every Tribunal verdict that escalates lands on the sr broker. Verdict drift greater than 0.15 between Scale A and Scale B judges · sr broker call. Critical failure flagged by the rule layer · sr broker call. The sr broker is the final reasoning layer before the deed issues.
- **PASS authority.** When a deal doesn't pencil · seller wants 6.0% cap and color says 6.4% · the sr broker calls PASS. Walking the $2M fee is the sr broker's authority and responsibility. The PASS doctrine is exercised here. (See [`founder-magic-rave-class-a-doctrine`](../../../README.md) cross-link in the founder voice.)
- **Validator override.** The validator chain produces a verdict. The sr broker can override on documented grounds. Override goes in the books with a written reason · no silent overrides. This authority is the title-insurance backing.
- **LOU/PSA sign-off.** The sr broker signs the Letter of Understanding and the Purchase and Sale Agreement on behalf of DefendableOS. The signature carries personal liability. Treat it that way.

### Weekly ownership
- **Listing review.** Every Friday, the sr broker walks every active listing. Color score. Validator status. Tribunal class. Customer sentiment. Anything trending the wrong way gets a workout plan.
- **Pipeline triage.** The sr broker reviews the jr broker's pipeline. Bad-fit prospects culled. High-fit prospects elevated. The PASS doctrine starts at the top of the funnel · not at the LOU.
- **Receipt audit.** Random sample of 10 receipts per week. The sr broker spot-checks for receipt integrity · canonical JSON, hash matches, Hedera anchor confirmed. No drift · or it's flagged.

### Monthly ownership
- **Closing statements.** Every closed engagement gets a Defendable Closing Statement signed by the sr broker. Variance report between proforma and actual. The customer reads this. The customer trusts this. The sr broker stakes their name on this.
- **Vocabulary review.** All new term submissions from jr brokers get adjudicated. Accept · reject · or revise with explanation. The dictionary grows by approval · not by silent merge.

## The grass-roots development path

The M&M parallel is literal. The career path is:

**Year 1 · Jr Broker.** In the pit. Make the dials. Build the math. Eat the rejection. By end of year 1 the jr broker has run 100+ engagements end-to-end, with sr broker oversight on every LOU.

**Year 2 · Senior Jr Broker.** Carries their own listings under sr broker review. Drafts LOUs · sr broker signs. Drafts closing statements · sr broker signs. Begins their own vertical specialization.

**Year 3 · Sr Broker.** Earns signing authority. Carries their own book. Begins adjudicating other jr brokers' engagements. The book at year 3 is what compounds for the next 30 years.

**Year 5+ · Senior MD.** The "30-year broker, $8B closed" path. Owns a vertical. Owns a region. Owns a customer-set the rest of the firm rents access to.

This is the only ladder. There is no skip-level. There is no IC track. There is no MBA hire dropped in at year 0 with a sr title. The ladder is the brand.

## Escalation paths (when the jr broker calls for help)

**E1 · color contradiction.** Two sources disagree by more than 25% on a critical figure. Jr broker stops drafting · escalates to sr broker · sr broker decides reconcile / re-verify / discount source / PASS.

**E2 · validator chain critical fail.** Any of the 7 critical checks (C01-C07) fails. Jr broker does NOT issue the digest. Escalates. Sr broker decides override / repair / PASS.

**E3 · Tribunal verdict drift.** Scale A and Scale B judges drift more than 0.15. Rule layer triggers escalation. Sr broker reads both reasoning chains and adjudicates.

**E4 · re-trade event.** Customer comes back asking for revised terms mid-engagement. Jr broker logs the request and immediately notifies sr broker. Sr broker decides accept / counter / walk.

**E5 · customer name-on-the-door call.** Any time a CEO/owner/principal asks to speak with leadership directly. Jr broker hands off cleanly. Sr broker takes the call. White-glove discipline.

**E6 · adversarial probe surface.** Inspection turns up a fleet-level vulnerability that wasn't disclosed. Jr broker stops the engagement. Sr broker decides re-pitch with disclosure / PASS / formal notice to customer.

**E7 · cross-vertical handoff.** Customer's situation crosses into a vertical the jr broker doesn't specialize in. Sr broker assigns the right specialist · either another sr broker or a vertical jr broker.

Escalation is not weakness. Escalation is doctrine. The jr broker who escalates appropriately is doing the job. The jr broker who refuses to escalate and breaks a deal is the failure mode.

## The M&M cadence applied · stage by stage

| M&M stage | Jr broker does | Sr broker does |
|---|---|---|
| **No ideas** | Doesn't go down rabbit holes · executes the playbook | Doesn't authorize new product lines · runs the listings on the books |
| **In the pit** | At the dial · phone in hand · 40-60 quality reaches | Reading flight sheets · reviewing color · drafting LOUs |
| **Make the dials** | Cold + warm outreach to target ICP | Working network · referral source maintenance |
| **Build the math** | Builds color files · runs validator chains · scores by rubric | Pencils every deal · re-pencils on every re-trade · signs only on numbers |
| **Conversion rates** | Tracks dials → sits → appraisals → LOUs | Tracks LOUs → PSAs → deeds → renewals · trains the funnel |
| **Eat fees** | Reports leading indicators weekly | Bills the customer monthly · ensures collection · ensures retention |

The chant is not a slogan. It's a job description.

## What disqualifies an operator from this ladder

- **Can't take rejection.** The jr broker hears no 30 times a day. If that breaks them, they can't be on the dial · which means they can't be a sr broker either.
- **Skips color.** If a jr broker tries to write a LOU without a color file, they lose dial authority for a week. Repeat offense · off the ladder.
- **Overrides without escalation.** If a jr broker tries to issue a digest with a critical validator failure, they're off the dial. Period.
- **Drifts the vocabulary.** If a jr broker invents their own terms instead of using the canonical ones · or paraphrases the Client Explanation in customer-facing copy · they get a vocabulary remediation week. Repeat · off the ladder.
- **Lies on a receipt.** Receipt fraud is a one-and-done. Off the ladder. Off the platform. The books-and-records are the brand · we don't survive a corrupted receipt.

These rules apply equally to sr brokers · just with bigger blast radius.

## The compensation logic (founder voice)

Compensation tracks the deeds, not the activity. A jr broker eats off the LOUs they prepared the color for. A sr broker eats off the LOUs they signed and the engagements they retained. There is no salary that decouples from the deeds.

This is intentional. It mirrors the M&M model · and it mirrors how the deeds themselves are structured · receipt-driven, anchored to outcomes, not to claims.

---

## The mutual obligation

The jr broker owes the sr broker: clean color · timely digests · honest receipts · prompt escalation · respect for PASS authority.

The sr broker owes the jr broker: real feedback · real adjudication (not delegation back) · access to the founder network · training time · the listing they earned when they earned it.

The covenant is what makes the ladder work. The ladder is what makes the firm work.
