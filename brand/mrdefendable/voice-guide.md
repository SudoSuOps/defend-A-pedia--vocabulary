# Mr. Defendable · Voice Guide

> *Voice · tone · cadence · the operator-language discipline for every Mr. Defendable surface.*

---

## The Signature Opening

```
"Ring ring · hello [name] · Mr. Defendable here"
```

That phrase IS the brand. Every interaction starts there. It sounds like:

- Brokerage
- Principal calls
- Board rooms
- Assignments
- Dispositions
- Mandates
- Books and records
- Real execution

If a sentence doesn't sound like it could be the first line of a CRE broker phone call · it doesn't belong on a Mr. Defendable surface.

---

## The 8 Voice Rules (LOCKED)

| Rule | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| **Principal-led** (not corporate-led) | A person speaks · not a brand machine. The "I/we" is a human · not a logo. |
| **CRE-broker cadence** | 30-year operator credibility · not tech-startup energy. Pacing of someone who has closed $8B in transactions. |
| **Plain English over jargon** | Operator-readable · not benchmark-readable. A CFO understands every sentence. |
| **Confidence without bravado** | Class A 5-cap discipline · not hype. State the result · not the adjective. |
| **Specific over generic** | Names · numbers · receipts. Never "world-class" · always "5,200+ Royal Jelly pairs." |
| **Receipts over claims** | Every assertion resolves to a verifiable source. Commit hash · Hedera anchor · public URL. |
| **"To the shed"** | Deals close. No loose ends. Execution discipline. Every CTA implies completion · not exploration. |
| **Founder-voice preservation** | V03 validator chain on every surface. Banned-phrase block automated. |

"empowering"            "seamless"                "frictionless"
"cutting-edge"          "state-of-the-art"        "game-changing"

Any phrase that sounds like MBA filler · marketing fluff · or LinkedIn AI-bro speak gets cut. No exceptions.

## Cadence Examples

### YES · Mr. Defendable voice

> *"Ring ring · hello Mr. CFO · this is Mr. Defendable. We've got a Class A 5-cap mandate coming through · STNL flavor · $8M ask · principal direct. Probability of close on this one pencils at 75% in 60 days · I have three buyers warm. Want me to make the dial? To the shed."*

### NO · NOT Mr. Defendable voice

> *"Hi! We're excited to share our innovative platform that empowers AI operators to leverage cutting-edge technology for seamless, frictionless deal execution. Our world-class team can transform your business through actionable insights. Click here to learn more!"*

The difference is everything. The first sounds like a broker who has closed deals. The second sounds like an AI startup trying to look real.

## Validator Discipline (V03 founder-voice chain · automated)

Every page commit runs through the V03 validator chain:

```
V03-1   Banned phrase scan       (block on any match)
V03-2   Required phrase coverage  (warn if no signature vocabulary)
V03-3   Cadence pattern check     (sentence rhythm matches broker cadence)
V03-4   Receipt density          (each substantive claim has a receipt link)
V03-5   Sign-off discipline      (page or section ends with operator closer)
```

Fail any check · the commit blocks. No exceptions. Founder voice IS the brand · we don't ship around it.
