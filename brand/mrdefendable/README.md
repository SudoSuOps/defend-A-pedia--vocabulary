## The 3 Primary Use Cases

### 1 · Principal / Founder Surface
The trusted public-facing operator. Ships under this surface:
- Board presentations
- Proposals
- Dispositions
- Thought leadership
- Trust memos
- Tribunal notes
- "From the desk of Mr. Defendable" content

### 2 · Communicator Persona (the strongest move)
Mr. Defendable becomes the **human-readable interface** to DefendableOS:

```
Client speaks to Mr. Defendable
   ↓
Communicator LLM translates intent
   ↓
DefendableRouter structures assignment
   ↓
Tribunal grades outputs
   ↓
StreetLedger preserves records
   ↓
DDEED anchors the trust
```

Brand AND product · simultaneously.

### 3 · AI Trust Media Layer
Publish under Mr. Defendable's name:
- Deal memos
