## Production Discipline (LOCKED)

> *"Don't overproduce it. The authenticity IS the moat."*

### YES
- Shed energy · founder energy
- Real conversations · live debugging
- Board-room style principal calls
- Real assignments · real stress · real recovery
- Unedited mistakes left in
- Audible typing · audible reading · audible thinking
- Minimal intro · zero "subscribe and like" outro

### NO
- Music beds (too marketing-podcast tropey)
- Heavy editing (kills authenticity)
- Polished intros / outros
- Sponsorships from VC-fund-of-the-month
- Guests who don't pass PASS doctrine
- Any production element that signals "this is an asset class with a budget"

## Format Templates

### Format A · "Cold Open Principal Call" (15-30 min)

Cadence:
```
[ring ring]
Mr. D:   "Hello dev · Mr. Defendable here · got a minute"
dev:     "Yes Mr. D · what's on the desk"
Mr. D:   "[principal problem · 1-2 sentences · the trigger]"
dev:     "[operator response · diagnostic · technical]"
[10-25 min of back-and-forth]
Mr. D:   "[the synthesis · the lesson · the doctrine takeaway]"
dev:     "Got it Mr. D"
Mr. D:   "To the shed. Mr. Defendable speaking out."
[end]
```

### Format B · "Live Debugging Session" (30-60 min)

Cadence:
```
[ring ring]
Mr. D:   "Hello dev · we got something broken"
dev:     "Tell me what's not working"
[live walkthrough · screen-share if video · audible typing OK]
[Mr. D narrates the principal-side context · dev narrates the technical]
[discovery moment · 'oh wait · the failure mode is...']
[repair attempt · Tribunal grade · what worked]
Mr. D:   "[lesson · why this matters · doctrine implication]"
Mr. D:   "To the shed."
[end]
```

### Format C · "Tribunal Verdict Walkthrough" (20-40 min)

Cadence:
```
[ring ring]
Mr. D:   "Hello dev · pick a verdict from streetledger today"
dev:     "Got one · DDEED-... · let me pull it up"
[Read the verdict · scores · evidence · classification]
[Mr. D challenges the verdict · dev defends · or they agree it was wrong]
[Lesson · pattern · what the Tribunal got right or wrong]
Mr. D:   "To the shed."
[end]
```

## Episode → Vocabulary Deed Pipeline (the compounding flywheel)

Every episode goes through the same pipeline:

```
1. Recording (raw audio / video)
2. StreetChat intake (capture + storage on streetchat.eth path)
3. Transcript generation (Whisper · or human transcription for low-quality audio)
4. Vocabulary extraction (operator terms candidates surface)
5. Tribunal grading (Honey · Jelly · Propolis classification per chunk)
6. StreetLedger deed (`DDEED-MEDIA-PITS-{ep_num}-v1` on Hedera)
7. defendapedia.eth vocabulary expansion (new DDEED-DOV-VOCAB candidates)
8. Training corpus inclusion (Honey + Royal Jelly tiers feed Communicator + SwarmCurator)
```

Each episode = 1 DDEED + N vocabulary candidates + N training pairs.

**This is the only podcast on the internet that produces a Hedera-anchored books-and-records trail of itself.** That meta-layer is the brand.

See `media-to-dataset-pipeline.md` for the full architecture.
